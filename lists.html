Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients;

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbsp butter
  • 2tbsp cream

Instructions;
  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream into a bowl.
  3. Once butter is melted, add the eggs and cream.
  4. Using a spatula, fold the eggs from the edge o the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette).
  5. Once done enjoy! :D
